NAME:
   uipath identity group - Manage groups in your organization

USAGE:
   uipath identity group

DESCRIPTION:
   Use these endpoints to manage groups in your organization.

COMMANDS:
   bulk-delete   Deletes multiple local groups from a partition
   create-group  Creates a new local group
   delete        Deletes a local group
   get-group     Get local or built-in group by partition and group Id
   get-groups    Get all local and built-in groups from a partition
   update-group  Updates a local group information.
