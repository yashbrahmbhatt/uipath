NAME:
   uipath identity group bulk-delete - Deletes multiple local groups from a partition

USAGE:
   uipath identity group bulk-delete [command options] [arguments...]

DESCRIPTION:
   OAuth required scopes: PM.Group or PM.Group.Write.

OPTIONS:
   --partition-global-id string (required)

   --group-ids string,string,...
      IDs of the groups to be removed
