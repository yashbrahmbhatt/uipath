NAME:
   uipath orchestrator business-rules share-to-folders - Makes the business rule visible in the specified folders.

USAGE:
   uipath orchestrator business-rules share-to-folders [command options] [arguments...]

DESCRIPTION:
   OAuth required scopes: OR.BusinessRules or OR.BusinessRules.Write.

   Requires authentication.

OPTIONS:
   --folder-id integer (required)
      Folder/OrganizationUnit Id

   --business-rule-keys string,string,...

   --to-add-folder-ids integer,integer,...

   --to-remove-folder-ids integer,integer,...
