NAME:
   uipath orchestrator robots toggle-enabled-status - Toggles the status of the robots (enabled/disabled)

USAGE:
   uipath orchestrator robots toggle-enabled-status [command options] [arguments...]

DESCRIPTION:
   OAuth required scopes: OR.Robots or OR.Robots.Write.

   Required permissions: Robots.Edit.

OPTIONS:
   --enabled boolean (required)

   --folder-id integer (required)
      Folder/OrganizationUnit Id

   --robot-ids integer,integer,... (required)
