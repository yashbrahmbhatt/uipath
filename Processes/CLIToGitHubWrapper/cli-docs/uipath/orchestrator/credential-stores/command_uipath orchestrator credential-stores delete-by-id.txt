NAME:
   uipath orchestrator credential-stores delete-by-id - Deletes a Credential Store.

USAGE:
   uipath orchestrator credential-stores delete-by-id [command options] [arguments...]

DESCRIPTION:
   OAuth required scopes: OR.Settings or OR.Settings.Write.

   Required permissions: Settings.Delete.

OPTIONS:
   --key integer (required)

   --force-delete boolean
