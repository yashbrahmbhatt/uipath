NAME:
   uipath orchestrator job-triggers - Initiators of jobs

USAGE:
   uipath orchestrator job-triggers

DESCRIPTION:
   Triggers are used to schedule the execution of processes.

COMMANDS:
   deliver-payload-by-inboxid     Deliver payload for trigger inboxId.
   get                            Gets JobTriggers.
   get-payload-by-inboxid         Get payload for trigger inboxId.
   get-with-wait-events-by-jobid  Gets Trigger option for a job instance along with wait event details    .
