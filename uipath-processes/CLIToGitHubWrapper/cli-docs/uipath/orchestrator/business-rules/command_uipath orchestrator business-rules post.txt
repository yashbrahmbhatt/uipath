NAME:
   uipath orchestrator business-rules post - Create Business Rule

USAGE:
   uipath orchestrator business-rules post [command options] [arguments...]

DESCRIPTION:
   OAuth required scopes: OR.BusinessRules or OR.BusinessRules.Write.

   Required permissions: (BusinessRules.Create).

OPTIONS:
   --business-rule string (required)
      The BusinessRuleDto object

   --file binary (required)

   --folder-id integer (required)
      Folder/OrganizationUnit Id
